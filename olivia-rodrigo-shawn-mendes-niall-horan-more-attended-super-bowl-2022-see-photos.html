Olivia Rodrigo, Shawn Mendes, Niall Horan &amp; More Attended Super Bowl 2022 See Photos!

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, July 13, 2024

There were so many celebs in attendance at the 2022 Super Bowl, including some top stars in the music world!

Olivia Rodrigo, Shawn Mendes, and Niall Horan were just some of the musicians who stepped out for the big game on Sunday night (February 13) at SoFi Stadium in Los Angeles.

Shawn and Niall sat side-by-side while watching the game and were also seen leaving together with their friends.

Demi Lovato was also at the game and they sat one row behind Keke Palmer, who stars in the new movie Nope. The trailer for the movie debuted in a commercial during the game!
