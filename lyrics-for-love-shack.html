Lyrics For Love Shack

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, September 16, 2024

Love Shack” is a song by the American rock band The B-52’s.

It was released in 1989 and became one of the band’s biggest hits, reaching number three on the Billboard Hot 100 chart.

The song’s upbeat tempo and playful lyrics about a rustic roadside cabin have made it a quintessential party anthem.
