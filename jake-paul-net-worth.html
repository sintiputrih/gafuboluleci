Jake Paul Net Worth : Career &amp; Lifestyle

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, June 16, 2024

The American YouTuber, Internet personality, and professional boxer, Jake Paul’s net worth is $20 million.

The YouTuber primarily rose to eminence through a social media platform, Vine, which he used to gain many followers.

He created his YouTube channel, Jake Paul, on May 15, 2014, and after Vine got shut down, Jake was able to appeal to those followers to his YouTube channel. The channel has 20.5 million subscribers as of his writing.

Just Like his older brother Logan, Jake is also involved in professional boxing.

Throughout his career, Paul has engaged himself in numerous controversies due to his behavior.

Following the controversies, he was fired from his Disney television show Bizaardvark. He was also charged with criminal trespass and unlawful assembly.

Jake Paul, originally Jacob Joseph Paul, was born on January the 17th, 1997, in Cleveland, Ohio, United States.

His father, Gregory Allan Paul, is a realtor however his mother, Pamela Ann Stepnick, is a nurse by profession.

He also has an eldest older brother named Logan Paul which is a YouTuber as well as a professional boxer.

Jake started his career in September 2013, posting videos on Vine.

By the time Vine was discontinued by Twitter Inc., Paul had amassed 5.3 million followers and 2 billion views on the app.
