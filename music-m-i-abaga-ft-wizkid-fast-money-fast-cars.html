MUSIC: M.I Abaga ft Wizkid Fast Money Fast Cars

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, March 21, 2024

Prolific Nigerian rapper, M.I Abaga pairs up with highly rated Nigerian singer, Wizkid to outdoor a beautifully crafted song titled; “Fast Money Fast Cars” off his album “Talk About It”.

The body of works came in a package of 15 solid and mind blowing tracks with vocal assistance from some of Africa’s most finest artists.

“Talk About It” album was released in 2008. M.I Abaga, real name Jude Abaga rose gain prominence with the release of his song; “Crowd Mentality”.

M.I Abaga bagged best hip hop act and best new act at the MTV Africa Music Awards in 2009. He was nominated in the Best International Act category at the BET Awards 2010.

M.I Abaga was the CEO of Chocolate City from June 2015 to 2019. He officially announced his exit from the collective in 2020. He later founded his record label; “Incredible Music”.

The “One Naira” hit-maker has released four studio albums namely; “Talk About It”, “MI 2: The Movie”, “The Chairman” and “A Study on Self Worth: Yxng Dxnzl”.
