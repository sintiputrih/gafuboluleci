Karim Benzemas Girlfriend: Who Is Jordan Ozuna?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, March 24, 2024

Karim Benzema is widely recognized as one of the world’s top soccer players, representing both Real Madrid and the French national team. Recently, he secured the prestigious Ballon d’Or award, the highest individual honor in the sport. But who is the woman who shares his life and success? Her name is Jordan Ozuna, a model, mother, and a recent convert to Islam.

The Love Story of Karim Benzema and Jordan Ozuna

Karim Benzema and Jordan Ozuna have been in a relationship since 2022. Their story began back in 2016 at an event in Los Angeles, where they first exchanged phone numbers. Benzema even openly expressed his admiration for Ozuna on Twitter, seeking advice from his followers on how to approach her.

Though they maintained contact for years, their relationship wasn’t publicly confirmed until December 2022 when they both posted photos on Instagram, showcasing their matching outfits and holding hands. Benzema captioned his post with “We just click!” and ” #thisismylove,” while Ozuna wrote, “Hearts are synched. My whole and actual love @karimbenzema.”

In 2023, the couple celebrated their birthdays together in March and August, respectively. Benzema shared a heartfelt tribute to Ozuna on her birthday, referring to her as a “Sunshine Human” and his “favorite playmate.” Ozuna reciprocated by expressing herself as the “luckiest woman alive.”

Their support for each other extended into their professional lives. Ozuna accompanied Benzema to the Ballon d’Or ceremony in Paris, where he received the award for his exceptional contributions to Real Madrid. Likewise, Benzema cheered for Ozuna during the launch of her new clothing line, Mami Wata, which celebrates African surf culture.

The couple is expecting their first child together, and Ozuna has chosen to take a break from her modeling career. She has also embraced Islam, the religion of her partner, Benzema.

Introducing Jordan Ozuna

Jordan Ozuna, aged 32, hails from Maryland but grew up in Hawaii and Las Vegas. Her career began as a waitress at Hooters, where she spent three years before relocating to Los Angeles to pursue modeling.

Currently, she is signed with several reputable modeling agencies, including State Management, Caroline Gleason Management, Tricia Brink Management, and The Salt Agency. Her modeling portfolio boasts appearances in prominent magazines like Maxim, FHM, and Sports Illustrated.

Beyond modeling, Ozuna is recognized as a TV personality and a designer, having hosted shows for MTV, E!, and Vice. She is also a co-founder of Mami Wata, an apparel company that celebrates African surf culture.

In the realm of music, she is both a musician and a producer, releasing two albums under the pseudonym Alekesam: “The Sound of Alekesam” in 2012 and “Sound Proof Heart” in 2018. Additionally, Ozuna has contributed to the music of various films and TV shows, including Entourage, House of Lies, and Insecure.

Ozuna is a mother to her son, Solal, from a previous relationship, and she has been linked to celebrities like Justin Bieber and P Diddy.

In Conclusion

Karim Benzema and Jordan Ozuna share a unique and inspiring bond, both finding success in their careers, embracing their African heritage, and enjoying adventurous pursuits. They transitioned from long-term friends to loving partners, and their evident happiness and support for each other promise a bright future for their relationship. We extend our best wishes to this extraordinary couple as they embark on this new journey together.
