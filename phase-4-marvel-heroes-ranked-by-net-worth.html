Phase 4 Marvel Heroes, Ranked By Net Worth

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, April 21, 2024

The Marvel Cinematic Universe has divided all of its productions into phases. Both the movies and now television shows released fall into category of Phase One, Two, Three, or Four depending on when it debuts. The current season is Phase Four, beginning last year in 2021 with Black Widow and WandaVision and ending in 2023 with the upcoming Blade and Fantastic Four films.

Related: 10 Marvel Movies Made Before Disney Bought The MCU

Thanks to the Disney+ streaming service, we have been introduced to many new heroes and storylines through the television series as well as the films. From first-time movies like Shang-Chi and the Legend of the Ten Rings and Eternals to sequels like Doctor Strange and the Multiverse of Madness and Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 3, fans everywhere will see their favorite supers again as well as meet new ones. Here are the new heroes of Marvel’s Phase Four ranked by their current net worth.

9 New Hero 'Ms. Marvel' Has A $500,000 Net Worth

Ms. Marvel is one of the newest miniseries available on Disney+ that takes place in the MCU. Kamala Khan, a teen who is obsessed with superheroes, unexpectedly finds herself with powers one day. This new character is played by Iman Vellani, who made her acting debut on this television series. Being so new to the craft, her net worth currently sits at around $500,000.

8 Upcoming Ant-Man Star, Kathryn Newton Is Worth $1 Million

Kathryn Newton is set to join the rank of heroes next year through Ant-Man and the Wasp: Quantumania as Cassie Lang, Ant-Man’s daughter. She has a net worth of $1 million thanks to her involvement in productions like Big Little Lies,Pokémon: Detective Pikachu, and Lady Bird. Newton will play the adult version of Cassie and follow in her father’s footsteps, donning a heroic suit of her own.

7 Lauren Ridloff, Of Eternals, Has A Net Worth Of $2 Million

Eternals brought several new faces to the Marvel Cinematic Universe. One of these heroes is Makkari, who is played by Lauren Ridloff. While this production is what boosted her notoriety as a movie actor, she was previously known for her role on the series The Walking Dead. At only twelve credits on her resume, she has made a name for herself with a net worth of $2 million.

6 She-Hulk: Attorney At Law Star, Tatiana Maslany's Net Worth Is $3 Million

Tatiana Maslany will make her Marvel debut in August this year through the TV series She-Hulk: Attorney at Law. Prior to becoming a super, Maslany has been cast in over 70 projects. Some of her most prominent are The Harper House, Orphan Black, and Perry Mason, which have raised her net worth to $3 million.

5 The Shang-Chi Hero, Simu Liu Is Worth $4 Million

Shang-Chi and the Legend of the Ten Rings hit theaters in September 2021, introducing us to Simu Liu as the titular character. He has a current net worth of $4 million. Outside his involvement with the MCU, he appeared in an episode of Star Wars: Visions, starred in Kim’s Convenience, and landed a role in Taken.

4 Gemma Chan, Of Eternals, Has A $6 Million Net Worth

Another star to come out of Eternals is Gemma Chan, who took center stage as Sersi. While she previously made an appearance in Marvel through Captain Marvel, Chan loved this chance to take a starring role, which helped elevate her net worth to $6 million. Besides this film, she can be seen in Crazy Rich Asians and Disney’s animated film Raya and the Last Dragon.

3 Florence Pugh, From Black Widow, Is Worth $8 Million

Florence Pugh is a beloved actress who has starred in many productions, from Midsommar to the 2019 Little Women to Fighting with My Family. She is even set to star in the upcoming Dune: Part Two alongside previous costar Timothée Chalamet. One of Pugh’s recent roles was Yelena in Marvel’s Black Widow, bringing her net worth to $8 million.

2 Hawkeye Star, Hailee Steinfeld Has A Net Worth Of $10 Million

To close out 2021, the Marvel Cinematic Universe released the miniseries Hawkeye on Disney+. Opposite Jeremy Renner as Hawkeye, Hailee Steinfeld starred as Kate Bishop. She is well-known for her many roles, such as Pitch Perfect, Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse, and Dickinson, not to mention her years-long music career. All of these accomplishments have boosted her net worth to $10 million.

1 Moon Knight Star, Oscar Isaac's Net Worth Is $12 Million

Moon Knight is a recent favorite among Marvel fans, airing its first episode in March of this year. Oscar Isaac showed off his immense talent as an actor, playing not one but two roles. He has been part of the Star Wars universe as Poe Dameron, the animated The Addams Family films, and Dune. Because of his wondrous skills, Isaac now has a $12 million net worth.
