Dominik Mysterio Tears Up Anti-Rhea Ripley Fan Sign During WWE Live Event

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, August 20, 2024

Dominik Mysterio wasn’t really liked when he was a plain babyface when he teamed up with his father Rey Mysterio. Things changed for Domink Mysterio after he turned heel last year and things have truly become better for him. Now it appears Dominik Mysterio tore up an anti-Rhea Ripley sign during a recent WWE live event.

Following a brief stint in jail as part of his storyline, Dominik Mysterio came back as Prison Dom. Fans and peers alike have been praising Mysterio’s current heel character for good reason.

Dominik Mysterio’s popularity is because of his excellent partnership with Rhea Ripley, as the two remain the biggest menaces in all of WWE right now. People also continue to ship them no matter what.

WWE recently held their Supershow in Charleston, West Virginia and Dominik Mysterio competed against his father Rey Mysterio. While he was making his way to the ring, Dominik noticed a fan sign in the audience that read ‘Di-A-Rhea Ripley’ and immediately tore it up.

Dominik Mysterio would end up losing to his father afterwards. Regardless, he is set to square off against Cody Rhodes at Money In The Bank on July 1st, so Dominik Mysterio has bigger things to think about.
