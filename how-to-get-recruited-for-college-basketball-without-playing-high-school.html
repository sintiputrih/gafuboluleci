How to get recruited for college basketball without playing high school?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, August 16, 2024

Basketball is a competitive sport that requires hard work, dedication, and talent to play at the college level. One of the most important steps in achieving this goal is getting recruited by college coaches and scouts. While high school basketball is a common path to college recruitment, it is not the only option available. However, it is important to note that this is a challenging path and requires a lot of hard work and dedication.

One key strategy for getting recruited without playing high school is to participate in AAU basketball. AAU basketball is a league for amateur basketball players, where teams compete in regional and national tournaments. Many college coaches and scouts attend AAU tournaments in search of talent, making it a great opportunity to showcase your skills and get noticed.

Another strategy is to participate in community college basketball.
