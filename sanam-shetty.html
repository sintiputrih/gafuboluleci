Sanam Shetty - Net Worth, Bio, Career, Single, Age, Facts

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, July 4, 2024

Table of Biography

Sanam Shetty is a famous Indian actress and model. Sanam Shetty is well recognized for her involvement in four South Indian film industries: Tamil, Telugu, Malayalam, and Kannada.

Early Life

Sanam Shetty was born on November 12, 1985, and she is currently 38 years old. Sanam is a native of Bangalore, India, and her zodiac sign is Scorpio. Talking about her family, her mother is Krishna Shetty, who comes from a Telugu family. Likewise, her father is Prasad Shetty who comes from a Tulu family.

Likewise, her full name is Sanam Prasad Shetty. Talking about her educational journey, Sanam attended Candor International School. Later on, she pursued a career in software engineering.

Sanam Shetty – Career and Professional Life

Professionally, Sanam is a famous actress and model. She has worked mostly on Tamil, Telugu, and Malayalam films. The popular actress initially developed an interest in fashion shows while studying computer engineering at college. In the mid-2000s, fashion choreographer Prasad Bidapa recruited Sanam as a model.

Soon, Sanam took part in the Miss Bangalore competition in the year 2004. However, she still chose to pursue a career in software engineering. As a software engineer, Sanam worked with companies like Tata Consultancy Services in the year 2006. Sanam moved to the United Kingdom for a short period. This was to continue working in software engineering.

During her time there, Sanam Shetty also worked on amateur documentary films. Through this, Shetty developed an interest in acting opportunities. The actress once again worked as a fashion model after choreographer Ganesh Gurung re-introduced her to Chennai’s fashion scene.

Modeling and Acting

Subsequently, Sanam became a model for several brands in the city during the weekends. She did this while still working as a software engineer on the other days. Then in the year 2010, The New Indian Express named Sanam Shetty and Arthi Venkatesh among the top models in Chennai, India.

Talking about her acting career, Sanam Shetty initially worked in the Malayalam film Cinema Company in 2012. However, the actress made her acting debut with Ambuli in the same year. It is a Tamil science fiction film. released in 2012.

Caption: Sanam during a cut from one of her Tamil movies. Source: Times of India

In the film Ambuli, Sanam plays the role of a young college girl who becomes caught up in mysterious happenings in a village. This film is set in the 1970s. Following this, the actress received numerous acting offers. Some projects that Sanam Shetty has worked on include the Tamil-Malayalam bilingual project Ravu and Maayai both released in 2013.

In the year 2014, Sanam worked on the film Vilaasam as well as a high-profile film Daivathinte Swantham Cleetus. Moreover, the actress signed on to play the lead role in Aabavanan’s female-centric film. She was set to play the role of a martial arts expert. However, this film was dropped later on.

More Acting Projects

Throughout the 2010s, Sanam Shetty continued to appear in small-budget films in the South Indian film industries. The actress’s notable Tamil films include Katham Katham, Vellaiya Irukiravan Poi Solla Maatan, and Sadhuram 2. Likewise, Sanam also played a supporting character in the Telugu film Srimanthudu, which also stars actor Mahesh Babu.

Likewise, Sanam also played the lead role in Singam 123 and Premikudu. Further, the actress worked on her 1st Kannada film Atharva in 2018. This film also stars the actor Pavan Teja. Then in the year 2017, Sanam launched her 1st film as a producer. It is titled, Magie. It is the tale of a girl brought up in Meghalaya.

Then in the year 2019, the Indian actress was belatedly crowned as the winner of the Miss South India 2016 beauty pageant. This was after the organizer opted to dethrone Meera Mithun. They dethroned Meera due to her fraudulent activities.

Similarly, the Indian actress became a participant in the reality TV show, Bigg Boss Tamil. It is a reality show where Kamal Haasan is the host. Sanam became a part of the show in the year 2020.

Sanam Shetty – Brand Endorsements

Being a popular actress, she has been involved in brand endorsements. She is the brand ambassador of many brands including jewelry, textile, and skincare products.

Net Worth 2024

Moving on, Sanam has a net worth of approximately $1.8 million US dollars.

Sanam Shetty – Relationship Status

She is currently single and more focused on her career. Previously, the actress was engaged to fellow actor Tharshan Thiyagarajah. The pair announced their engagement in May 2019. They had even planned a wedding for June 2019.

However, her then-fiance’s involvement in Bigg Boss Tamil in 2019 postponed their wedding. There are many questions among the fans about whether she is married or not. However, the couple canceled the engagement in late 2019, and subsequently, their marriage was also canceled. Later on, Sanam filed a police complaint against her ex-fiancee for misusing her funds.

Body Measurements

She stands tall at an approximate height of 1.67 meters or 5 feet 6 inches and her weight is around 54 kgs. Likewise, the Indian actress’s chest-waist-hip measures 34-26-35 inches. Sanam has dark brown hair and brown eyes.
