Billie Eilish biography

Billie Eilish, one of the biggest stars in the United States, was born in Los Angeles, California, on December 18, 2001. She is the offspring of Eilish’s touring musicians Patrick O’Connell and Maggie Baird, an actress and teacher.

Eilish is of Irish and Scottish ancestry and was created through in vitro fertilization.

Her middle name, Pirate, was supposed to be her first name, and her middle name, Eilish. She was raised in the Highland Park neighborhood of Los Angeles.

At the age of eight, Eilish joined the Los Angeles Children’s Chorus and made appearances on talent shows.

She started learning how to play the ukulele when she was six years old. When she was 11 years old, she composed her first “real” song for her mother’s songwriting class. She drew inspiration for the song, which is about a zombie apocalypse, from The Walking Dead and used script lines and episode titles in its composition.
