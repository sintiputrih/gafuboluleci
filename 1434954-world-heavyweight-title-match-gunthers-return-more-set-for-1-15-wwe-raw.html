World Heavyweight Title Match, Gunthers Return, More Set For 1/15 WWE RAW

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, July 28, 2024

WWE is loading up the card for the January 15 episode of WWE RAW.

In the likely main event, Seth Rollins will defend the World Heavyweight Championship against Jinder Mahal. The match was announced after Mahal interrupted Rollins on Monday night.

WWE also announced that Gunther will make his return. The reigning WWE Intercontinental Champion took some time off following the December 18 episode. There, he defended the title by defeating The Miz.

Plus, The Judgment Day will compete in two tag team matches. Dominik Mysterio and JD McDonagh will take on DIY (Tommaso Ciampa and Johnny Gargano). Meanwhile, Undisputed WWE Tag Team Champions Finn Balor and Damian Priest will face The Miz and R-Truth.

WrestleZone will have full coverage of WWE RAW as it airs on January 15.

Updated Card For 1/15 WWE RAW

The announced card for the January 15 episode of RAW is as follows:

  • : Seth Rollins (c) vs. Jinder Mahal
  • Finn Balor & Damian Priest vs. The Miz & R-Truth
  • DIY (Tommaso Ciampa & Johnny Gargano) vs. Dominik Mysterio & JD McDonagh
  • Gunther returns

WrestleZone will have coverage of the show as it airs on Monday.
