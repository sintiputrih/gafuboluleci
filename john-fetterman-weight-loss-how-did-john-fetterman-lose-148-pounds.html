How did John Fetterman lose 148 pounds?

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, February 18, 2024

Pennsylvania Lt. Gov. John Fetterman’s campaign team revealed on Thursday (May 19) that he raised over $2 million dollars just a day after winning the Democratic primaries to compete for the state’s all-important open U.S. Senate seat.

John Fetterman weight loss: How did John Fetterman lose 148 pounds?

The 52-year-old received those contributions despite being sidelined by a stroke last week, which kept him off the campaign trail in the final days of the primary. On the day of the election, he had undergone his surgery successfully.

Another topic of discussion is Fetterman’s weight. how he got his new refined appearance. It all started with his mindset, he claims. He went on to say that if you want to achieve anything in life, you must change your thoughts.

Fetterman added the next step was changing the way he ate, cutting out grains and sugar. He started walking more and dropped about 10-12 pounds a month. Today, he is down to 270 pounds.
