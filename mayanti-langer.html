Biography/Wiki

Mayanti was born on 8 February 1985 in Delhi, India. As her father Sanjiv Langer was an army man in Indian Army, she brought up in a cantonment and used to do various sports activities in her childhood. During this time, she developed an interest in sports, especially in football.

Physical Appearance

She is a talented and beautiful lady with a fit physique of 36-32-38 and 5′ 6″ height. She has dark brown eyes and black hair.

She belongs to a Christian Indian family in which her father Sanjiv Langer is an Indian Army Personnel and mother Preminda Langer is a teacher.

Career

Mayanti is graduated in Arts from the Hindu College in New Delhi. After graduation, she was pursuing the masters in arts. At this time, officials of Zee Sports came to know about her through the Indian Youth Soccer Association (IYSA) league in Delhi, that she used to run a few years ago, and they called her for an audition. After being selected and successful performance, she continued her journey in this field.

Mayanti Langer

In 2010, her first broadcasting on TV was as an anchor for the FIFA beach football league. As a host, her first live event was the football Federation Cup in Goa. Despite being nervous and inexperienced, she got into the action, and took it as a conversation among her group of friends over a particular topic, without fearing from the cameras or millions of viewers.

Mayanti Langer

Till now, she has hosted various tournaments-  2010 Commonwealth Games, 2010 FIFA World Cup (broadcast on ESPN), 2011 Cricket World Cup, First Indian Super League in 2014, and 2015 ICC Cricket World Cup. She has hosted 6 World Cups across 3 different sports successfully. She has also worked as an associate producer of a football show that she used to anchor for Zee Sports.
