Meet Robby Toma, Manti Te'o Friend! His Bio, Wife, IG, Job Now

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, May 24, 2024

When everything around the life of Manti Te’o was crumbling with family and personal crises, one person who never left his side was his best friend Robby Toma.

Untold: The Girlfriend Who Didn’t Exist is a roughly two-hour-total documentary that examines the story and aftermath of the Punahou School graduate’s early 2010s rise as a Notre Dame linebacker, fan favorite, and Heisman Trophy finalist while he was simultaneously hooked into a long-distance relationship with a fictional woman, Lennay Kekua.

“The documentary showed us all how strong Manti was and has always been. But the picture of us three playing video games struck a cord. 3 Laie Boyz in the Mid West did it together,” after the show premiered on Netflix, Robby posted on his IG. “This picture is how I will always remember South Bend. Wish you were still here Kona Geezy so you could find the McChickens. NEVER ABOVE YOU, NEVER BELOW YOU, ALWAYS SIDE BY SIDE.”

So, who is Manti’s dear friend? Learn more about his age, job, wife and kids, and family in this article below.

Netflix’s The Girlfriend Who Didn’t Exist: Meet Robby Toma, Manti Te’o Friend

Robby Toma is a childhood friend of Manti Te’o who is the subject of the two-part Netflix show Untold: The Girlfriend Who Didn’t Exist. Robby grew up four houses down from him Manti who joined him with the Irish as a slot receiver. Together they played in Punahou High & at Notre Dame.

While they thrived as an athlete together, Robby also supported Manti as much as he can. Speaking on the Netflix show, Robby opened up that Manti was having a hard time adjusting so he never gave thoughts to his long-distance relationship, as long as it was keeping him happy.

Robby knew that Te’o hadn’t seen this girl yet, he also knew that long-distance was not that uncommon, considering he was in one himself. When the “girlfriend” scandal came to light, Manti informed his best friend not to get involved in it. Robby wanted to defend his friend by speaking publicly, but Manti shared that the matter at hand was his alone to deal with.

“I talked to him the night everything kind of unfolded and he was really high in spirits. That’s the kind of person he is, that’s how he was raised, that’s how we all were raised. It was frustrating because of all people I knew the truth because I know Manti better than anyone else,” Robby told Baltimore Sun.

Moreover, he maintained that his friend was a good person who look out for others. “He told me to stay out of the media and that he’ll do it on his own. It was frustrating being his best friend and not being able to help him. You know how it is in the media. It was real bad. It was definitely tough, but he’s a great person,” he said.

Robby Toma Age

Robby Toma’s date of birth is 23 February 1991. In February 2022, Robby celebrated his 31st birthday.

Robby Toma Job Now

According to Robby Toma’s LinkedIn profile, he is working for Monarch Insurance Services, Inc. as a Producer, VP & Account Manager. He joined the company in February 2019. In February 2022, Robby announced on his Facebook, “A year ago I left a very great opportunity and began a new journey with Monarch. I am proud of the growth but I know I have so much more to accomplish professionally. I took a gamble on myself and I am excited about the future. If anyone has ANY insurance-related questions or concerns. Please don’t hesitate to reach out to me.”

From 2013 to 2019, Robby worked at Universal Property & Casualty Insurance Company as Claims Adjuster.

Moving on to the details regarding his education, Robby attended Punahou School and graduated in 2008. He played football in high school and was named 2008 Hawaii Co-Offensive Player of the Year by the Honolulu Advertiser. He was also named first-team all-state by Advertiser.

Robby led the state with 1,393 receiving yards and 15 receiving touchdowns.

Moreover, Rivals.com ranked him the eighth-best player in Hawaii and named him one of the top 150 players in SuperPrep[apos]s Far West (California, Hawaii, Nevada) rankings.

Later, Robby enrolled at the University of Notre Dame to study communication and media studies. He had also enrolled in the College of Arts and Letters as a film, television, and theatre major. Learn more about his collegiate football career here.

Is Robby Toma On IG And Facebook?

Indeed, Robby Toma uses Instagram (@r.toma9), Twitter (@rtoma9), and Facebook (@RobJob293).

Robby Toma Wife

Robby Toma is currently in a relationship with his partner, Kaui Robello. They can be seen together on Instagram as early as December 2018 having dinner with Robby’s family members. On Kaui’s birthday in March 2020, he wished her via an IG post while writing, “Wanna wish this BABE a very happy birthday!! Thank you for all that you do for Mila & I. We love you very much!”.

Kaui was born on 23 March 1988 which makes her 34 years old. She was born to her parents Rodney and Alicia Robello and has two brothers Lopaka and Kaipo.

Kaui is also a graduate of Punahou School, class of 2006. She studied psychology at Siena College. She is working at Taormina Sicilian Cuisine. All through high school and college, she played volleyball. 

Robby is a father of two kids; Mila & Jett. Mila was born on 25 May 2019. She is three years old in 2022. Meanwhile, Jett was born on 21 May 2021. In 2022, he is 1 year old.

Robby Toma Family

Robert “Robby” Joseph Toma is the son of Tammy and Ross Toma. On Father’s Day 2016, he wished his dad, “Happy Father’s Day to the man who never let me down. Always pushed me to be my best. Always helped me up when I was down. I hope I can be half the man he is. Enjoy your day dad! Hope uncle James doesn’t beat you up on the golf course too bad today! Love you old man!”.

Robby has an older brother named Ken Toma. In December 2016, he wished Ken his birthday, “Happy Birthday to my brother Ken. Now instead of baby bottles we have switched over to beer bottles. 🍺 Love you bro, have a good one!!”.

More about his family, he lost his grandfather sometime in 2006. Other family members of Robby are Fran and Frances.
