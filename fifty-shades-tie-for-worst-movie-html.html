Fantastic Four and Fifty Shades of Grey Tie for Worst Picture at the Razzies

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, May 9, 2024
Take comfort in your massive domestic gross.

On the eve of the Oscars, the Razzie Awards blow one fat raspberry in the face of Hollywood with their not at all daring selection of the worst films of the year. This year, Fantastic Four and Fifty Shades of Grey tied for Worst Picture, while Fifty Shades also received Worst Actor, Worst Screen Combo, Worst Screenplay, and Worst Actress (seriously, did they watch the movie? Dakota Johnson is the best thing about it). Josh Trank, who publicly disavowed the final cut of Fantastic Four, won Worst Director for the superhero feature, which also took home Worst Remake. Since the Razziesâ€™ generosity is boundless (and also passive-aggressive), they gave Oscar nominee Slyvester Stallone the Razzies Redeemer award for getting his acting together in Creed.
