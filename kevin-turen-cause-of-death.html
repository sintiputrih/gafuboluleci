Kevin Turen Cause of Death: Producer of Euphoria and The Idol, Dies at 44

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, May 7, 2024

Kevin Turen, a film and television producer who worked on Euphoria, The Idol, and other projects, died unexpectedly over the weekend. He was 44 years old at the time. You will find all of the details of his demise on this page.

A Prolific and Passionate Producer

Turen was a productive and enthusiastic producer who worked in a wide range of genres and forms. He began his career as an assistant to producer Scott Rudin before co-founding Before the Door Pictures with actor Zachary Quinto and producer Neal Dodson.

Margin Call, All Is Lost, A Most Violent Year, and The Invitation are among the films he produced under this banner. Turen has also worked on multiple projects with filmmaker Sam Levinson, including the HBO series Euphoria, starring Zendaya, and the Netflix film Malcolm & Marie, starring Zendaya and John David Washington.

He also produced Levinson’s next film, The Idol, a Sia biopic starring Kate Hudson and Maddie Ziegler. Turen was also in films including Arbitrage, 99 Homes, Assassination Nation, and The Climb. He won a Gotham Independent Film Award for Best Feature for Margin Call in 2011 and was nominated for an Emmy Award for Outstanding Limited Series for Euphoria in 2020.

A Loving Husband and Father

Turen has been married to celebrity cosmetics artist Evelina Oboza since 2012. Henry and Oscar were their two boys. Turen was a devoted husband and father who cherished his time with his family and friends.

He was also an avid traveler and adventurer who enjoyed learning about different cultures and places. Many people in the entertainment world and beyond have been startled and saddened by Turen’s passing.

On social media, his colleagues and partners have expressed their sorrow and condolences, remembering him as a creative, giving, and kind guy who made a great impression on everyone he met. Turen’s career and family will carry on his legacy. All who knew and loved him will miss him tremendously.
