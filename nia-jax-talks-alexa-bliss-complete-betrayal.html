Nia Jax Talks Alexa Bliss Complete Betrayal

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, August 24, 2024

Nia Jax and Alexa Bliss have a very strong history with each other and Jax’s Raw Women’s Title win at WrestleMania was a huge turning point in the story for Jax and Bliss.

The Rock’s cousin recently told the story of her and Alexa Bliss’ and how betrayed she feels via an Instagram slideshow where she said:

“Once upon a time… I had a best friend. SOmeone I trusted thoroughly & genuinely loved. I never imagined things would turn out the way they have. She has completely betrayed and hurt me. She has thrown every insecurity she knew I had in my face. She USED me & now she is playing the victim during her Moments Of Bliss. I am done playing this game & tomorrow at Backlash I’m going to end it.”

It looks like the Raw Women’s Title match is a bigger deal than just being Alexa Bliss’ first match back since her recent cosmetic surgery. How would you book Alexa Bliss vs Nia Jax?
