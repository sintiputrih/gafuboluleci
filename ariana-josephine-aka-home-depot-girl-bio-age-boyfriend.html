Meet Ariana Josephine aka The Home Depot Girl

Many social media users praised Ariana Josephine for pursuing academics and other opportunities to grow her money rather than becoming a model and commented on her beauty on Twitter. She recently sat down with Distractify and spoke, “I was shocked because it was a joke and [it] actually blew up.”

Texas-based Ariana claimed that she started working at the home improvement company three months ago in order to take advantage of the perks, which include a 401(k). The NBA legend Shaq is said to have Dm’ed the viral Home Depot girl, which helped her become an overnight internet hit. But it’s not what you may expect. The screenshot shows a cheeky comment, “Have a great day tell all your boyfriends I said hello.” Ariana replied with a “HAHA I will thank you.”

Some social media users assumed right away that Shaq was attempting to take a shot at the beautiful influencer. But according to a post by DJ Akademiks, the former basketball player soon set the record straight.

Shaq’s reaction to the circumstance is depicted in Akademiks’ post. The Hall of Famer reportedly responded to Akademiks’ remark on whether or not he was attempting to hit on the Home Depot girl by saying, “Just lifting people that’s what the diesel do, baby I uplift people google me.”

“I thought it was cool to see someone inspirational give me kind words because I actually wasn’t taking all the attention too well at that moment,” she told Distractify.
