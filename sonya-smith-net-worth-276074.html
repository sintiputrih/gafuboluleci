Sonya Smith Net Worth: Sonya Smith is an American-born Venezuelan actress who has a net worth of $5 million. Sonya Smith was born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania in April 1972. She is best known for her roles in telenovelas. Smith has starred in the TV series Gardenia, El desprecio, Cara sucia, Rosangelica, Maria Celeste, Guajira, Destino de mujer, Milagros, Marlu, Olvidarte jamas, Pecados Ajenos, Tiempo final, Acorralada, Vuelveme a querer, ?Donde esta Elisa?, Aurora, Corazon Valiente, Secreteando, and Marido En Alquiler. She has also starred in the films Un sueno en el abismo, Cyxork 7, Under the Same Moon, Ladron que roba a ladron, Hunted by Night, and Unknowns. Sonya played actress Mariana Torres' mother in four different telenovelas. Smith won a Latin Pride National Award for Outstanding Woman Achievement in 2008 and a Miami Life Award in 2011.
