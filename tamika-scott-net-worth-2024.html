Early Life and Career Beginnings

Tamika Scott was born in College Park, Georgia, and showed an interest in music from a young age. She, along with her sister LaTocha, formed the R&B group Xscape in the early 1990s. The group was discovered by Jermaine Dupri, who helped them secure a deal with So So Def Recordings.

Rise to Stardom with Xscape

Xscape released their debut album “Hummin’ Comin’ at ‘Cha” in 1993, which went platinum. The group enjoyed considerable success throughout the 90s, releasing three platinum albums and numerous hit singles. Their success significantly contributed to Tamika’s net worth.

Solo Career and Acting

After Xscape disbanded in 1998, Tamika embarked on a solo career. She released her debut solo album “The Movie” in 2019. Besides music, Tamika also ventured into acting, appearing in plays like “Meet the Browns” and “Love Overboard,” further increasing her wealth.

Reunion with Xscape

In 2017, Xscape announced their reunion, which was a significant event for Tamika both personally and financially. The group embarked on a nationwide tour, released new music, and starred in a reality TV show, “Xscape: Still Kickin’ It.”
