Cannes Film Festival Winners Announced (Updating Live)

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, April 23, 2024

CANNES — The awards show for the 75th anniversary Cannes Film Festival is underway, bringing 12 days of competition between 21 international features to a close.

The jury for the official selection was led by French actor Vincent Lindon, who won Cannes best actor honors in 2015 for “The Measure of a Man.” Lindon co-presented the awards with fellow jurors Iranian director Asghar Farhadi, English actor-director Rebecca Hall, French director Ladj Ly, American director Jeff Nichols, Indian actor-producer Deepika Padukone, Swedish star Noomi Rapace, Norwegian director Joachim Trier and Italian actor-director Jasmine Trinca.
