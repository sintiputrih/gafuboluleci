It’s Cinco de Mayo 2022!

Celebrated annually, the Mexican holiday commemorates Mexico’s victory over the French Empire at the Battle of Puebla in 1862.

Cinco de Mayo is honoured every year on May 5th and the phrase actually translates to “Fifth of May” in Spanish.

Widely celebrated in America and Mexico, people often eat Tacos, drink Tequila and have parties with friends and family.
