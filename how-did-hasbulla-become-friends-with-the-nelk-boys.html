How Did Hasbulla Become Friends With the NELK Boys?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, February 25, 2024

By Jocelyn Alano June 26, 2024 17:57

credit. Full Send Podcast and NELK

Hasbulla Magomedov, a 21-year-old Russian social media sensation, has captured the hearts of millions worldwide with his infectious laughter and charismatic personality. Standing at just 3'4 feet tall, Hasbulla, who has dwarfism, became an internet sensation in 2021 when a TikTok video of him went viral, propelling him to stardom.

 

The Rise of Hasbulla

Hasbulla's rise to fame didn't stop at TikTok; he quickly gained attention for his unique personality and ability to connect with others, garnering a massive following of over eight and a half million fans on Instagram. His journey from a restrictive school environment to the creative freedom of the Internet allowed him to showcase his resilience, humor, and authenticity, transcending the limitations he faced.

 

Hasbulla's Connection with the UFC

In October, Hasbulla made headlines by signing a five-year deal with the UFC, marking his presence in the mixed martial arts scene. His appearances at high-profile UFC events and his growing association with the organization signify his rising popularity and the broader acceptance of his unique presence within the MMA community. This journey from social media sensation to a visible figure in the UFC highlights the evolving landscape of entertainment and sports, where unconventional paths lead to unexpected opportunities.

 

Friendship with the Nelk Boys

Recently, the Nelk Boys had a one-of-a-kind experience in Russia, where they met with Hasbulla and Abubakar Nurmagomedov. A video released on Instagram by Full Send MMA captured the boys' surprise as they witnessed the Russian rashly driving past the cops, with Hasbulla in the car having laughing fits. Despite the shocking act of 'Manap' pretending to throw Hasbulla out of the window, the fans couldn't help but adore Hasbulla's adorable laughter.

The Unlikely Friendship

Hasbulla's journey from Russia to becoming friends with the Nelk Boys, known for their outrageous stunts and pranks, showcases the power of social media in bridging unlikely friendships and creating memorable moments. Despite the language and cultural barriers, their shared sense of humor and lighthearted interactions have brought them together, making for entertaining and heartwarming content that resonates with fans worldwide.

 

In the End

Hasbulla's remarkable journey from a small town in Russia to global stardom, his unlikely friendship with the Nelk Boys, and his growing association with the UFC highlight the transformative power of social media and the universal appeal of authenticity, humor, and resilience. As Hasbulla continues to break barriers and make a name for himself, his story serves as an inspiring reminder that size doesn't limit one's ability to make a significant impact and form meaningful connections in the world of sports and entertainment.
