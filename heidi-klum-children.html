Heidi Klum Children: A Brief Look At Heidi Klums Family

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, March 20, 2024

The April 2022 issues of Harper’s Bazaar Germany feature individual covers featuring Heidi Klum, 48, and her daughter, 17, respectively.

Heidi is shown in a close-up on the cover of the book, laying her head on a stone by what appears to be a beach. Her neatly manicured nails, smokey eye makeup, bare lips, and bright yellow outfit are all highlighted in the photo.

The cover of the magazine, however, showed Leni Klum putting a hand on her cheek while gazing intently into the lens, her dazzling blue eyes capturing the entire spotlight.

Heidi, who typically shields her kids from the spotlight, posted the Leni-adorned cover on her Instagram grid with the following message in German:

“LENI [red heart emoji], you inherit my mother’s gorgeous blue eyes.”

A brief look at Heidi Klum’s family

After divorcing her stylist husband of five years, Ric Pipino, in 2002, Heidi Klum began dating millionaire Italian Formula 1 racing executive Flavio Briatore. Leni, Flavio’s daughter, was born to Heidi in May 2004, not long after the couple split.

Heidi was dating British singer-songwriter Seal (real name Henry Olusegun Adeola Samuel) when Leni was born; they later got married in 2005. Leni was adopted by Seal in December 2009.

Leni made her modelling debut in December 2020 when she appeared with her well-known mother in the January/February 2021 issue of Vogue Germany.

Heidi posted a picture of the book’s cover to her Instagram account and added a German caption:

“I’m very proud of you, and not only because you chose this road; I know it will be your own no matter which way you choose.”
Henry Günther Ademola Dashtu Samuel, the supermodel and singer’s first child, was born in September 2005. The second son of the pair, Johan Riley Fyodor Taiwo Samuel, was born in November 2006.

Lou Sulola Samuel, the third and final child Heidi has biologically with Seal, was born in October 2009. In January 2012, Heidi and Seal divorced, and the divorce was finalised three months later. In October 2014, the divorce was legally finalised.

The conservative mother-of-four German-American model subsequently dated Vito Schnabel, an art curator, for two and three years, respectively, from 2012 to 2017.

Heidi revealed her romance with German guitarist, musician, and composer Tom Kaulitz in May 2018. The couple, who was married in February 2019, is apparently still together as the artist forges relationships with Heidi’s kids.
