Goldie Hawn, 70, Models Slim Body in Tight Nude Swimsuit

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, April 6, 2024

Goldie Hawn is 70 years old — and not a day over fabulous! The actress and mom of three showcased her slim figure in a skintight nude swimsuit while enjoying the shores of Maui, Hawaii, on Thursday, June 16.

PHOTOS: Hot Celebs Over 40 in Swimsuits

The Oscar-winning actress worked her body in the taupe one-piece, which boasted a V-neckline and dainty spaghetti straps. She also rocked embellished sunglasses and a chocolate-colored scünci. After she took a dip in the ocean, Hawn also added a sky-blue striped maxidress.

PHOTOS: The Hottest Celeb Bikini Bodies

The mom of Kate Hudson, 37, Oliver Hudson, 39, and Wyatt Russell, 29, was vacationing with Wyatt and his girlfriend, Meredith Hagner. (The pair met on the set of Folk Hero & Funny Guy.) All three were spotted splashing in the ocean then enjoyed a stroll on the sand.

On how she’s managed to age so gracefully (and zen-ly!), Hawn told Porter magazine, “It’s wonderful to know you’re aging, because that means you’re still on the planet, right?” She added, “It’s all about how you make it. It’s all in your mind.”
