My keys to the kitchen

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, September 9, 2024

I’m really excited (ok, totally flattered), to be on Aida Mollenkamp’s site today with a little interview sharing my keys to the kitchen.

I always loved watching Aida on the Food Network, and I’m so excited to meet up with her this weekend while she’s in town for her Keys to the Kitchen book tour. She’s in Dallas and Houston today and tomorrow, but she has a really cool event happening in Austin this Sunday night at Hotel St. Cecelia. I think there are a few tickets left… if you live in Austin, you should come.
