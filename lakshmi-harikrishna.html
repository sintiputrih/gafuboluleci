Her husband’s career

Nandamuri Harikrishna was a talented movie actor, producer, and also politician whose work is quite popular all over his nation. He was a part of the Parliament of the Rajya Sabha from Andhra Pradesh.

The actor was also popular for his good performances in Telugu cinema. Nandamuri was the 4th son of N. T. Rama Rao who was a Telugu matinee hero and earlier chief minister of Andhra Pradesh.

In the year 1964, Nandamuri Harikrishna made his first acting appearance as a child artist.

There, he was present in “Sri Krishnavataram” with the role of Krishna. The director of the same film was Kamalakara Kameshwara Rao. In the year 1970, the actor got a role in “Thalla? Pellama?”.

Likewise, the actor acted in other films such as “Tatamma Kala” in the year 1974, “Ram Raheem” in the year 1974, and “Daana Veera Shura Karna” in the year 1977.

After a lengthy leave and political appearance, Nandamuri performed as a Character actor in “Sri Ramulayya” in the year 1998.

Then, the actor got a lengthy role together with Nagarjuna in “Seetharama Raju” in the year 1999, “Lahiri Lahiri Lahirilo” in the year 2002, and “Seetayya” in the year 2003.

In the year 2002, the actor got the Nandi Award in the category of Best Character Actor for his performance in “Lahiri Lahiri Lahirilo”.

Other films of the actor are ” Tatamma Kala”, “Daana Veera Soora Karna”, “Swamy”, “Sravanamasam”, ” Hamari Ranbhoomi”, ” Siva Rama Raju”, and “Tiger Harischandra Prasad”.

Moreover, the actor also worked as a member of the Andhra Pradesh Legislative Assembly from the year 1996 to the year 1999. The actor turned out to be a Cabinet Minister for Road Transport in the year 1996.

In the year 2008, Nandamuri Harikrishna was a nominee of the Telugu Desam Party and was even chosen to Rajya Sabha. Unfortunately, the actor passed away in a car accident on 29th August 2018 when he was just sixty-one years old.
