RHONJs Teresa Giudice Set For Pregnancy: Joe Wants A Baby Boy

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, June 18, 2024

Is Teresa Giudice, of Real Housewives of New Jersey fame, going to have another baby?  If hubby, Joe, has any say in the matter her eggs will be fertilized stat.  After giving birth to and raising four daughters, the reality star is considering having another Giudice offspring, only this time the pair are hoping for a boy.

Though Teresa was adamant about not having any other babies, Joe hinted at the idea of surrogacy.  Teresa doesn’t want to carry another child, fine.  She can be just like every other VAIN celebrity who care more about her body than the gift of life and look for a gestational surrogate to carry the load.  It doesn’t seem to make any different to Joe as long as he gets his boy.

The idea of a baby boy is not new to the couple.  In fact, it was rumored that Teresa’s jealousy reached epic proportions when her arch enemy and sister-in-law, Melissa Gorga, gave birth to a son.  Teresa loved her nephew tremendously and that made her want to have a son of her own.  She underwent in vitro fertilization, but ended up pregnant with another girl.  After that, Teresa swore she was done.

The thought of a son is still in the back of the duo’s mind as they have reportedly and prematurely picked out a name for their son, Franco.  Teresa wanted to pay homage to her father in law by naming a son after him.  A very sweet gesture, but it looses it’s meaning if you have no intention of having any more kids.

What do you think readers?  Will the Giudice’s bite the bullet and have another baby?  Will Teresa give in and carry her child herself or do you think they will start their search for a surrogate?  Hey, it could lead to a pregnancy special for them.
