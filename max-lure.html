The Max Lure (Japanese: ゴールドコロン Gold Cologne) is a type of item introduced in Pokémon: Let's Go, Pikachu! and Let's Go, Eevee!.

The Lure and Super Lure are weaker versions of this item.

Effect

When used from the Bag in the overworld, it increases encounter rates of rarer wild Pokémon, including Shiny Pokémon, for 250 steps. It also causes all wild Pokémon spawned to have a level 1 greater than the highest level normally in the area (for example, if an area's wild Pokémon normally range from level 3 to level 5, all wild Pokémon will be level 6 instead).
