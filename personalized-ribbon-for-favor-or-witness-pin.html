Personalized ribbon for martyrika or bomboniera Blessed Celebration

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, July 8, 2024
PLEASE NOTE: THERE IS A MINIMUM ORDER OF 20 RIBBONS ON THIS ITEM.

A customized personalized ribbon for your use in decorating a witness pin or favor. Choose your own color ribbon and font color.

  • Ribbon width: 3/8" (1 line of text per side.) or 5/8" (up to 3 lines of  text per side.)
  • Ribbon colors available in a good range of colors, sure to suit every occasion

** To best assess the ideal length, we recommend taking a string or dental floss and making a mock sample of what you are using the ribbon for.  You can then measure the string or floss once done to get the needed length.
