Priya Taneja, popularly known as Honeypreet Insan whose skills are not just limited to acting, yet she is also a great director as well. She was born in 1975, Fatehabad, India. Being a star, she is very active on all her social media accounts where she loves to interact with her fans and followers. She is the daughter of Ramanand Taneja and Asha Taneja. Preet has three brothers named Sahil Taneja, Amanpreet Kaur Insan, and Jasmeet Singh Insan with whom she is so attached. Also, she has a sister named Nishu Taneja. Honeypreet did her graduation and later, she discovered her interest in acting.

Career

Honeypreet Insan is famous for her directorial debut “MSG 2- The Messenger” which was released in the year 2015. In the movie, she also appeared as a lead actress for which she got immense love and appreciation from everyone. Later, she worked in “The Worrier Lion Heart” and many more. Meanwhile, she also directed the movie “Hind Ka Napak Ko Jawab” and was honored with more than 20 awards for the same movie. Apart from this, she has worked in many movies as an actress and is also known for her editing skills. Additionally, Honeypreet loves going gym as she is a fitness freak. She is so popular on social media as she has a huge fan base following on all her accounts including Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, and others. However, she is one of the finest stars who flaunts her incredible talent.

Achievements

Among various notable achievements, Honeypreet Insan was honored with more than 20 awards for her directorial “Hind Ka Napak Ko Jawab”.

Personal Life

11 years ago, Honeypreet Insan got married to a person named Vishwas Gupte with whom she is living a happy married life.
