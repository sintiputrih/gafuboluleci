Jonathan Cheban Shows Off Kim Kardashians Massive Christmas Tree

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, May 3, 2024

https://www.instagram.com/p/BOFy62rgZJE/

Christmas, Kardashian-style!

On Friday, December 16, Keeping Up With the Kardashians costar Jonathan Cheban swung by his BFF Kim Kardashian’s palatial pad to check out her and husband Kanye West’s enormous flocked Christmas tree.

In the entrepreneur’s video, his longtime pal cannot be seen, but Kim — who has been lying low on social media since her armed robbery in Paris in October — she does chime in about her merry decor.

“Isn’t it insane?” Kardashian West asks in the background. “Just lights, no ornaments.”

While it’s unclear exactly how tall the tree is, it certainly looks enormous (par for the course at a Kardashian Christmas) behind Cheban.

Whispering, since the couple’s children North and Saint were sleeping, Cheban narrates the Snap. “Guys, look at the size of this tree. Only a Kimye tree could be the size of a tree in Rockefeller Center. This is crazy. Holy crap,” he says.

The Kardashian Wests aren’t the only ones in the family with epic holiday decor. Kylie Jenner showed off her similarly large and flocked Christmas tree in an Instagram post earlier this week.

https://www.instagram.com/p/BOAvkFOBANc/

Kendall Jenner also shared her white Christmas tree on Instagram, but it was decidedly smaller than her siblings’.

The entire Kardashian clan has always done Christmas big, including momager Kris Jenner’s annual Christmas Eve party — which even brought out Drake last year.

Kris’ ex Caitlyn Jenner has previously applauded the get-together.

“Her Christmas Eve party is over the top spectacular,” Jenner said last year on her website. “She does such a beautiful job with decorating the house. We’ve had the same Santa Claus for 25 years.”
