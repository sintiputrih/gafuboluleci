Who is Amanda Seyfried? | Amanda Seyfried Biography and Wiki

Amanda Seyfried is an is an American actress, model, and singer who became famous in her role as Karen Smith in the teen comedy Mean Girls (2004) and as Lilly Kane in the TV series Veronica Mars (2004–2006). Seyfried started modelling at 11 years and acting at 15 years. 

Amanda has credit for her supporting roles in her role as Samantha in Nine Lives (2005), Julie Beckley in Alpha Dog (2006) and Sarah Henrickson in Big Love (2006-2011). When Seyfried began acting, she portrayed the role of Lucy Montgomery in As the World Turns (1999-2001), a soap opera that was aired on CBS and Joni Stafford in All My Children (2003), a soap opera that was aired on ABC.

Amanda Seyfried Age and Birthday

Amanda was born on December 3, 1985, in Allentown, Pennsylvania, United States of America. She celebrates her birthday on December 3rd every year.  Seyfried will be 35 years old on Dec. 3rd 2020.

Amanda Seyfried Height and Weight

Amanda is a woman of average stature, she also appears to be quite tall in stature in her photos. She stands at a height of 5 feet and 3 inches (1.60 meters). She also weighs 108 lbs (49 kgs).

Amanda Seyfried Education

Amanda studied and graduated from Allentown’s William Allen High School in 2003. After graduation, she enrolled at Fordham University, however, she did not attend as she was offered her role in Mean Girls.

Amanda Seyfried Family

Amanda mother is Ann (née Sander), an occupational therapist, and her father is Jack Seyfried, a pharmacist. Her family is majorly of German descent with a bit of Scots-Irish, English and Welsh ancestry. Seyfried has a sister, Jennifer Seyfried.

Amanda Seyfried Husband

Amanda is married to Thomas Sadoski. Thomas and Amanda co-starred in the comedy and drama film, The Last Word. Seyfried and Sadoski engaged on September 12, 2016. The couple tied their knot in March 2017 in a private ceremony and together they are blessed with one child namely, Nina Sadoski Seyfried. The family resides in New York.

Amanda Seyfried Boyfriend

Before marrying Thomas Sadoski, Amanda dated celebrated stars including Desmond Harrington, Dominic Cooper and Justin Long. Amanda had a well-known relationship was with Dominic Cooper his co-star in Mamma Mia from 2008 to 2010. Seyfried and Cooper two years relationship was on and off and eventually, they broke up in 2010.
