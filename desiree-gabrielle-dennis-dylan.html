Desiree Gabrielle Dennis-Dylan is the daughter of an American singer and songwriter, Bob Dylan.

Her Family

Desiree is the only daughter of the parent’s Bob Dylan and Carolyn Dennis. 

She has five half-siblings from the paternal side. 

They are Maria Dylan, Sam Dylan, Jesse Dylan, Jakob Dylan, and Anna Dylan. Maria is adopted by Bob Dylan. 

She is the daughter of Sara Lownds from his first marriage. 

Desiree also has one half-sibling named Crystal Faith from the maternal side.

Love-Life And Marriage

Desiree is a lesbian. She is married to Kayla Sampson. 

Desiree and Kayla met for the first time while Desiree was performing in the Young Americans, which is a traveling non-profit performing arts club for young people. 

The love-duo walked through the aisle on June 6, 2014, at Long Beach, California.

Her Parents Love-Life And Marriage

Desiree parents, Bob and Carolyn, dated for a year before getting married on June 4, 1986.  

Carolyn is Bob’s former backup singer. 

Their relationship was hidden from the world to protect their daughter, Desiree, from the media limelight. 

The couple wanted her to be able to live a normal childhood. 

Their relationship was revealed in 2001 through Howard Sounes‘ book ‘Down the Highway: The Life of Bob Dylan.’ 

The love-duo were together for six years and got divorced in October 1992. 

The reason behind their split has not been disclosed yet. 

Her Father Past Relationship

Desiree’s father, Bob, was previously married to former playboy bunny and model, Sara Lownds. 

The pair dated each other for almost three years and tied the wedding knot on November 22, 1965. 

Sara shares five children from her husband, Bob. The pair got separated after 12 years of their marriage in 1977.
