Who's In The Cast Of The Challenge: All Stars Season 2?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, August 14, 2024

Fan favorite Challengers return for another chance to take home the $500,000 grand prize. 

After a widely successful first outing, the powerhouse series The Challenge: All Stars is returning for Season 2 on Thursday, Nov. 11. The 10-episode long season will drop weekly on Thursdays, exclusively on Paramount+.

Hosted by TJ Lavin, this all-new season welcomes back 24 of the fiercest reality titans from the hit franchise's long history, including some who haven't competed in more than 20 years. 

STREAM: Full Episodes Of The Challenge: All Stars Season 1 On Paramount+

With past relationships that run deep and competitive streaks that never die, they will have to overcome the obstacles both in and out of the game to take home the win. 

Returning for a chance to win the ultimate competition, the players will face unprecedented, over-the-top challenges and vie for their shot at the $500,000 prize.

Meet The Challenge: All Stars Season 2 contestants:

- Ayanna Mackins — Seasons 4, 6, 9

- Brad Fiorenza — Seasons 9, 10, 11, 13, 15, 17, 18, 20 (winner), 31, 32

- Casey Cooper — Seasons 12, 13, 15, 18

- Cohutta Grindstaff — Seasons 16, 18, 25, 27

- Darrell Taylor — Seasons 7 (winner), 8 (winner), 10 (winner), 12 (winner), 18, 19, 29, 30, 36

- Derek Chavez — Seasons 20, 23, 24 

- Derrick Kosinski —  Seasons 9, 10, 11, 12, 13, 14 (winner), 16 (winner), 18 (winner), 20, 30

- Janelle Casanave — Seasons 14 (winner), 15

- Jasmine Reynaud — Seasons 21, 22, 23, 24, 25

- Jodi Weatherton — Seasons 10, 11 (winner), 13 (winner)

- Jonna Mannion — Seasons 21, 23, 24, 25, 26

- Katie Doyle — Seasons 7, 8 (winner), 9, 11, 12, 15, 17, 18, 20

- Kendal Darnell — Season 8 (winner)

- Laterrian Wallace — Seasons 4, 6, 7

- Leah Gillingwater — Season 8 

- Melinda Collins — Seasons 12, 15, 20, 23

- MJ Garrett — Seasons 11 (winner), 15, 17

- Nehemiah Clark — Seasons 13, 15 (winner), 17, 21

- Ryan Kehoe — Seasons 12, 15, 16, 17, 19

- Sophia Pasquis — Season 9

- Steve Meinke — Season 7

- Teck Holmes — Season 3, All Stars 1

- Tina Barta — Seasons 7, 9, 10, 12, 13

- Tyler Duckworth — Seasons 13, 15, 20 (winner), 21 (winner)

Multiple seasons of The Challenge are currently available on Paramount+ and don't miss The Challenge: Spies, Lies, and Allies every Wednesday at 8:00 PM, ET/PT on MTV.
