Disney Channel Stars Then &amp; Now: Photos Hollywood Life

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, January 25, 2024

We can’t believe how much time has passed since we hunkered down and watched some of our favorite DCOMs and Disney Channel classics. The stars of these series and movies have gone on to bigger things since their time under the Mickey Mouse moniker and it’s so exciting to see how far they’ve come. Some stars have gone on to become bankable Hollywood heartthrobs like Zac Efron. Others have made huge strides in their personal endeavors and been incredibly honest about their transformations into young adulthood, like Miley Cyrus.

Most of all, we feel like we’ve grown up alongside these stars! Even Selena Gomez who starred on Disney’s ‘Witches of Waverly Place’ and went on to be a huge recording artist. Selena has also been in some artistic films like 2012’s Spring Breakers and ‘The Fundamentals of Caring’ (2016). Selena has a huge fan base that consistently support her creative endeavors whether they are in front of the microphone or in front of the camera.
