Food Network host Esther Choi gives birth to first child and reveals babys gender, sweet name and

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, January 25, 2024

FOOD Network host Esther Choi has given birth to her first child.

Esther, 38, made the announcement with a photo of her baby boy Leonardo on her Instagram.

In the heartwarming snapshot, she laid down with her newborn son and planted a sweet kiss on his beanie.

The hostess revealed her baby boy was born on February 22nd.

"Sharing the very first photo ever taken of us," the Food Network host captioned the post.

"The minute he was born. Welcome to earth, my little dragon angel bebe Leonardo."

OTHER PROJECTS

The TV personality has shown off her cooking skills in Iron Chef: Quest for a Legend, Chopped, Battle of the Brothers, Throwback with Michael Symon, and Bobby's Triple Threat.

Esther is also the owner of the restaurant, Mökbar, and a partner of the restaurant Ms. Yoo.

She became the host of the Hot Ones spinoff, Heat Eaters.

In July 2023, Esther opened up to InsideHook.com about her obsession with spicy food.

"I think it comes from my background growing up in a Korean American household eating spicy food," she shared.
